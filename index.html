Bihar Diwas 2025: Celebrating the Glorious Heritage of Bihar

Bihar Diwas, observed on March 22nd every year, marks the foundation day of the state of Bihar. It commemorates the day in 1912 when Bihar was carved out from the Bengal Presidency as an independent province. This special day is a tribute to the glorious past, cultural richness, and immense contributions of Bihar to India.

Historical Significance

Bihar has been a land of knowledge, wisdom, and historical importance since ancient times. It was home to some of the greatest scholars and leaders, including Chanakya, Aryabhata, Guru Gobind Singh, and Dr. Rajendra Prasad. The state has also witnessed the rise of great empires such as the Maurya and Gupta dynasties, under which India flourished as a global power.
